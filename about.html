About PDF Tools Pro

Our Mission

PDF Tools Pro was founded with a simple mission: to provide professional-grade PDF manipulation tools that are completely free, secure, and accessible to everyone. We believe that essential document processing tools should not come with expensive subscriptions or hidden costs.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike many online PDF tools that limit functionality, add watermarks, or require registration, PDF Tools Pro offers 20+ professional tools completely free. Our key differentiators include:

  • 100% Free: No premium tiers, no hidden payments, no credit card required.
  • Privacy First: All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer.
  • No Registration: Use all tools immediately without creating an account.
  • Dark Mode: Work comfortably with our built-in dark/light theme toggle.
  • Professional Quality: Powered by industry-standard PDF libraries for reliable results.

Our Technology

We leverage cutting-edge web technologies including PDF-lib, PDF.js, and modern browser APIs to deliver professional PDF processing directly in your browser. This client-side approach ensures your documents remain private and secure while providing instant processing without upload delays.

Our Tools

Our comprehensive suite of 20+ tools covers all common PDF operations:

  • Conversion Tools: PDF to Word, Excel, JPG, and reverse conversions
  • Organization Tools: Merge, split, rotate, and reorder PDF pages
  • Security Tools: Password protection, unlocking, watermarking
  • Optimization Tools: Compression, repair, and metadata management
  • Advanced Tools: OCR, annotation, document comparison

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuously improving our tools and adding new features. Our development team regularly updates the platform with the latest PDF processing capabilities and security enhancements. We welcome user feedback and suggestions for new tools.

Security & Privacy

Your document security is our top priority. Because all processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript, your files are never uploaded to any server. This means:

  • Your confidential documents stay on your device
  • No file size limits (limited only by your device memory)
  • Instant processing with no queue times
  • Complete privacy - we never see your documents
