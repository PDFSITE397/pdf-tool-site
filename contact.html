Frequently Asked Questions

Are the tools really free?

Yes! All our PDF tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, no premium tiers, and no watermarks.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

What file size limits do you have?

Since files are processed locally, limits depend on your device's available memory. Most modern devices can handle PDFs up to 100MB easily.

Do I need to create an account?

No account or registration is required. Simply visit our site and start using any tool immediately.

How do I report a bug?

Use the contact form above with "Bug Report" as the subject. Please include steps to reproduce the issue and your browser/device information.
