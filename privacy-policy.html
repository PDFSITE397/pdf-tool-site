Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At PDF Tools Pro, we take your privacy seriously. Unlike most online PDF tools, we have designed our platform to process all documents locally in your browser. This means your files never leave your device, and we never have access to your documents.

Data Collection

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your uploaded PDF files or their contents
  • Converted or processed documents
  • Any personal information from your documents
  • File names, metadata, or document properties

We DO collect limited anonymous data:

  • Page views and tool usage statistics (anonymous, no personal identifiers)
  • Browser type and version for compatibility improvements
  • Device screen size for responsive design optimization
  • Dark/light mode preference (stored locally on your device)

How Local Processing Works

All PDF manipulation is performed using JavaScript libraries that run entirely in your web browser. When you upload a file:

  1. The file is read into your browser's memory
  2. Processing happens locally using your device's CPU
  3. The result is saved back to your device
  4. All data is cleared from memory when you close the page

No data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage (not cookies) to save your theme preference (dark/light mode). This data never leaves your browser. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses no external analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking scripts. The only external resources are the PDF library CDNs (Content Delivery Networks) which are necessary for functionality. These CDNs may collect standard server logs, but no file content is ever sent to them.

Children's Privacy

Our services are suitable for all ages and do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Since we collect no personal data, there is no personal data to access, delete, or export. You have complete control over your documents as they never leave your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us.

Data Security

While we cannot guarantee absolute security (as no system can), our local-processing approach eliminates the primary risk of data breaches associated with cloud-based PDF tools. Your documents remain on your device under your control at all times.

International Users

Our service is available worldwide. Because we process no data on our servers, there are no international data transfers to worry about. You maintain full ownership and control of your documents.
